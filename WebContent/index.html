About Us

DeSouza's School is a premier Institutuion being one of the oldest schools in Rourkela. founded in the year 1958 by Late Capt. John DeSouza and Late Mrs. Anne Desouza. The School is a registered body recognized by the Govt. of Orissa and affiliated to the Council for the ISC Examinations, New Delhi, sponsoring students for the ICSE and ISC Examinations.

The School, situated on a 6 acre campus at the foot of the Durgapur Hill Range, Sector-2 Rourkela, is one of the cleanest and greenest campuses with its gardens, lawns and playgrounds. In keeping with its motto "Born For Greater Things", the School caters to the all round development of the child through its band of well qualified and experienced Staff, extra-curricular activities, good academic results and infrastructure. The goal of the school seeks to equip the students with the intellectual and practical skills that are necessary to meet the inevitable challenges of the future.

FROM THE PRINCIPAL'S DESK

VISION

To create a community of learners who will develop into quality citizens of the world, nourish in a safe and trusting environment conducive to successful teaching and learning, so that they are the pride of the global community.

MISSION

To provide a safe and stimulating environment to impart holistic and quality education which encourages students to be life long learners, develop positive attitude, leadership qualities, global vision, critical thinking and stimulate respect and humanity for and towards others.
